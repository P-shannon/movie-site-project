Chronicles of a Cursed Soul:

Angelite's Story

In Theaters

05/02/2080

Synopsis

A scout and aspiring explorer of a small oasis town, the young Angelite Entyshak has had a fairly simple life. During a routine scouting run, she is captured by a cult and imbued with a sigil that will fix an otherworldly monster's focus to taking her body and destroying her soul. Learning that the horror will not stop pursuing her until she or it is dead, Angelite has to flee the beast and find leads that will help her remove the sigil or slay the beast.
